Simpletask Revived is a maintained Android fork of Simpletask for people who want a straightforward todo.txt workflow.

The revived fork keeps the familiar plain-text task model while establishing its own app identity, support path, and release process.

Current release focus:
- Cloudless local-file workflow
- todo.txt-compatible task editing
- Filters, sorting, reminders, widgets, and quick capture
- Local-first usage with external sync tools if you want them

This fork is being prepared for F-Droid with distinct application IDs and fork-owned support links.

Repository and issue tracker:
